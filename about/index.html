WHO EVEN IS 3HOLLIS?

3hollis is a frequency, a future echo, a little blip on your timeline that refuses to stay quiet. Born from the chaotic static left behind by 2hollis, the triple iteration adds an extra dimension of shimmer, sincerity, and a suspicious amount of green. This site is the official archive of that energy: music ideas, visual experiments, digital glitter, all packed into one web 1.0 inspired temple.

If you hear a synth squeal at 333hz while a blinking gif nods in approval, that's probably us. If you see a merch drop that feels like it time-traveled from 1998, also us. The goal is simple: make the future feel handmade, loud, and a little too earnest. You're invited to poke around, leave a guestbook scribble, and remember that the weird corner of the internet is thriving.
